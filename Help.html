Where is my order?
The value of your order will depend on how your order was sent and if tracking is available.

Orders under £75

If your order has been shipped using Royal Mail untracked service, please allow 5 working days (not including Sundays, National and Bank Holidays) from dispatch as some orders may take a little longer to deliver, if you have waited 5 working days and you still don't have your order, please contact us. For International orders, please allow 21 days for delivery, if you have not received it after this time please contact us. Please note: For pre-orders please allow 5 working days from release date.

Orders £75 and over
For orders shipped via our tracked services, you can use the tracking link in your shipping email or the link below that applies to track your order.
Orders £75-£149.99 - Hermes
Orders over £150, or with upgraded delivery - DPD

In the unlikely event that your order has been lost in transit, we will do our best to replace it as soon as possible (subject to product availability). Please be aware that you must contact us within 60 days of the despatch date of your order if it hasn’t arrived via the postal service. Unfortunately, claims for lost in transit orders after this time cannot be accepted.


How do I spend my gift card online?
You can use your gift card for full or part payment of any products (except pre-orders) on our website. The total will be taken from the balance on your gift card when you place your order.

To spend your Gift Card online;

Add item(s) to basket
Proceed to Checkout
In the discounts section select ‘Redeem A Voucher’, Enter the 19-digit number gift card number on the rear of the card and 4-digit PIN from your gift card (found under the scratch-off foil panel) click Apply and continue the checkout process.
